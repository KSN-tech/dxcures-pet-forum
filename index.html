Common skin conditions

Allergies Itching, redness, licking, ear irritation, or seasonal flares after food or environmental exposure.
Dermatitis Inflamed skin that may appear red, flaky, moist, smelly, or sore around folds, belly, chin, or ears.
Hot spots Fast-forming moist patches that can become painful, infected, and larger after scratching or chewing.
Mange Mite-related irritation that may cause crusting, intense itch, and patchy hair loss needing vet testing.
Fungal infections Ring-shaped bald or scaly areas can be fungal, but look-alike conditions are common.

Call a veterinarian sooner

  • Rapid spreading redness, swelling, or pain
  • Open wounds, pus, bleeding, or strong odor
  • Fever, low energy, or appetite changes
  • Severe scratching, head shaking, or sudden hair loss
Forum posts can help organize questions, but a veterinary exam is the next step for concerning signs.
