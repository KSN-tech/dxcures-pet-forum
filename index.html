Care guide

Helpful details to include before owners reply

Clear context helps other pet owners share more relevant experience. It also gives your veterinarian a better picture if you decide to book a check-up.

1

Describe the skin

Note redness, flakes, smell, wet patches, bumps, wounds, or hair loss.

2

Share recent changes

Mention food, shampoo, grooming, medication, outdoor exposure, or stress.

3

Track the timeline

Say when it started, whether it spreads, and what makes it better or worse.
